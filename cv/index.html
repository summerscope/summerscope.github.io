  • rateitapp.com

    RateIt is a startup capturing customer experience feedback via tablets in-store as well as after purchase through channels like web and SMS.

    My work included introducing a user research practice into the company for the first time, and introducing new methods to include design research into the product decision making cycle. I also managed and art directed a rapid redesign of the marketing site.

    I joined CoinJar as a contractor in order to help them complete the implementation of their new high frequency trading platform for professional traders: CoinJar Exchange. During my time there I helped with the completion and testing of the remaining feature sets, as well as some UX redesign work to make the navigation more scalable and robust.

  • factil.io

    Factil is an early stage technology startup introducing a new platform to automate and improve data modeling, based on Fact-based modeling, targeted at enterprise and government with large data silos. In 2017 Factil received a government grant to create a POC search tool for child safety databases (which are not accessible nationally). Read more about the BRII project.

    My role covers UX design and front-end implementation for product, as well as branding and messaging for Factil. I'm working with the team to establish efficient cadences for feature development, to improve and refine sprint planning and agile processes. Introducing the team to UX and design thinking methodologies in a series of workshops has successfully drawn out assumptions held by individual team members. Additionally, it has helped us rapidly express feature ideas by building up capability and confidence to draw and ideate visually, which helps us understand and clarify ideas much more rapidly than with conversation alone (I'm trying to make "drawing what you mean" a thing 😁).

  • clover.com.au

    User experience & product designer, plus front-end developer for fin-tech startup. Clover is a robo-investor bringing simpler, smarter investing to the Australian market.

    During my time at Clover, I introduced and refined a number of UX research activities, including guerrilla style usability testing and deeper user interviews to better understand the attitudes and emotions of our target demographic.

    Other wins include a significant refactor of the front end codebase, implementing a truly component-driven development workflow, and exploring and selecting a living style guide rendered directly from the codebase. This style guide became an invaluable development tool, as it provided the perfect place to develop components before they were used in views, as well as providing an easy way to compare component implementations and avoid issues like unnecessary duplication.

    In addition to improving the maintainability of the codebase, our refactor significantly improved the consistency and usability of the product overall. We had significant positive feedback on the the usability of the tool which was a direct response to this UX and FED work.

  • medtasker.com

    Product designer & front-end developer for med-tech startup, Medtasker, a platform for mobile and web task management in hospitals. Worked across several aspects of the business, and led the design effort for the marketing site, the explainer video, the help & documentation site, as well as the actual product design.

    During the design phase, conducted ethnographic research and user interviews to better understand the hospital technology landscape. During product trial, performed training and support services for the ward staff, and improved UX iteratively based on user feedback. Design focus on optimising usability for first-time or naive users, while still supporting power-users.
