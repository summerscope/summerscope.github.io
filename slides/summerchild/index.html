Before we convince others...

we must first convince ourselves

As technologists, we must get better at forming our own opinions on the kinds of risk and degree of risk in the systems we build
  • FairLearn
  • Aequitas
  • Deon
There are a bunch of tools out there for helping you measure unfairness against protected classes in a rigorous statistical way. Do data auditing. Or provide a data ethics checklist. But all these tools are deep in the weeds on the ML implementation or statistical methodology and don't really help you form an intuition how your system's failure modes will translate in the real world.
